# Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD) and the Complement System

## Main Topic
- **Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD)**
  - Chronic & Progressive Degenerative Disease of Retina
  - Leads to Blindness
  - Mainly Affects Elderly Population

## Pathogenesis & Pathophysiology
- **Complexity**
  - Structural & Cellular Complexity of Retina
  - Variety of Risk Factors & Molecular Mechanisms
- **Risk Factors**
  - Genetic Predisposition
  - Natural Aging Changes
  - Lifestyle Factors (Smoking, Nutrition)
- **Mechanism Understanding**
  - Not Fully Understood
  - Drug Discovery is Challenging

## Genetic & Molecular Studies
- **Complement System**
  - Important Player in AMD
  - Genetic Risk Variants Cluster in Complement System Genes
  - Complement Activation Products Elevated in AMD Patients

## Complement System Role in AMD
- **Complement Activation**
- **Inflammation**
- **ECM Stability**
- **Energy Metabolism**
- **Oxidative Stress**

## Therapeutic Strategies
- **Regulating Complement System**
  - Focus of Therapeutic Pipelines & Clinical Trials
  - Attempts Not Yet Fully Successful
